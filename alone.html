Alone

I sing and sing
and from within
I wish and wish
for a mate to come.

To build and build
a nest with chicks
and greet the day
with song.

Between trees and trees
of brick and stone
where tin cans scream
and no one sleeps.

I stand and stand
tall and strong
on the corner
of my dwelling.

Where I sing and sing
and from deep within
I wish and wish
for my mate
to finally come.
