Sonder II

I walk my dog at night.
There's an apartment building
just around the bed.
Big and looming,
lit with pretty lights.

Blue lights in one room,
a movie night, perhaps?
One glows dim and golden,
a cozy night on the couch.
Another stirs with perfume,
work clothes,
or the nerves of a first date.
Maybe someone spilled a drink
and is mopping it up.
Someone fell asleep
in front of the tv.

A hundred windows,
some alight some dark.
A hundred different tales
as vibrant and alive
as yours and mine.
Eight billion little stories
in each of them,
someone shines.
